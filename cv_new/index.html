I am a research scientist in the Fundamental AI Research (FAIR) group at Meta in NYC and study foundational topics in machine learning and optimization, recently involving reinforcement learning, control, transport, flows, and diffusion. My research is on learning systems that understand and interact with our world and focuses on integrating structural information and domain knowledge into these systems to represent non-trivial reasoning operations.
